Vegan Breakfast Burritos

These vegan breakfast burritos feature an eggy tofu scramble, vegan bacon, vegan sausage, crispy roasted potatoes, and a selection of fresh veggies. All rolled up into a flour tortilla and griddled to perfection. No one will believe they're vegan!

Cooking Details

Course: Breakfast, Lunch, Main Course

Cuisine: Mexican, Vegan, Vegetarian, Comfort Food, High Protein

Prep Time 30 minutes

Cook Time 40 minutes

Total Time 1 hour 10 minutes

Servings 6-8 servings

Roasted Potato Ingredients

  • 2-3 large red or white potatos(washed and diced into 1/2-1/4 inch cubes)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1-2 tbsp garlic powder (to taste)
  • 1-2 tbsp onion powder (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp mixed herbs
  • Sea salt and ground black pepper (to tase)

Tofu Scramble Ingredients

  • 400g extra firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1-2 tbsp garlic powder (to taste)
  • 1-2 tbsp onion powder (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp mixed herbs
  • 1 tbsp ground tumeric
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose seasoning
  • 1 tbsp powdered chicken/veggie stock
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1-4 tbsp sriracha mayo (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1 heaped tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp dijon mustard
  • 2 heaped tbsp nutritional yeast
  • Sea salt and ground black pepper (to tase)

Burrito Ingredients

  • 1 large diced truss tomato
  • 1/2 diced red onion
  • 8 pack soft tortillas
  • 2 heaped handfuls of baby spinach
  • 4 vegan sausages (for this recipe we used Quorn brand, however please use your brand of choice)
  • 4 slices of vegan bacon (for this recipe we used Next! brand, however please use your brand of choice)
  • Optional additions: sliced avocado and vegan cheese

Instructions

Roasted Potato:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C / 430F (fan-forced).
  2. Cut potato into bite size pieces and add to a large bowl. (click link to see how to properly prepare/cut potato)
  3. Add the olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, mixed herbs, salt and pepper and toss to combine until evening coated.
  4. Transfer coated potato to a large baking tray, lined with baking paper. Roast for 30-40 minutes, tossing half way.
  5. Once slightly charred and fork tender, remove tray from oven and leave aside.

Vegan Meats:

  1. Place the 4 vegan sausages on a lined baking tray and cook at 220C / 430F for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Cut vegan bacon into small bite sized pieces and add to a lightly oiled pan, frying until cooked, but not crispy (this is important as it will further cook later, and if it is over-cooked to begin with it will go very tough).
  3. Transfer bacon to a plate/bowl with paper towl to soak up residual oil and set aside.
  4. Once sausages are cooked, cut in half, then further halve each half and dice into small (1/4 inch) bit sized pieces. Set aside with bacon.

Tofu Scramble:

  1. Whilst the potato is cooking, remove the extra firm tofu from its packaging and crumble in your hands directly into an oiled pan. (please note this receipe does not require you to press the tofu beforehand). The crumbled tofu should have no large chunks and should resemble scrambled egg in size/shape.
  2. Turn the pan onto a medium heat. Add all spices/sauces/condiments to the tofu scramble and stir thoroughly to combine.
  3. Once evenly coated, cook for 5-10 minutes on low-medium heat.

Burrito:

  1. Whilst tofu scramble is cooking, dice tomato and red onion.
  2. Once tofu scrmable is cooked, add to pan:
    • Diced red onion
    • 2 generous handfuls of baby spinach.
    • Diced tomato.
    • Cooked potato.
    • Cooked sausage.
    • Cooked bacon.
  3. Cook the mixture 3-5 minutes, ensuring mixture is combined. Remove from heat.
  4. Heat tortillas according to packet instructions, then take an individual tortilla, add 2-3 heaped spoonfuls of the mixture to the centre, then proceed to wrap the burrito. (Optional: add avocado, vegan cheese or whatever sauces/extras you prefer.)
  5. Grill/toast the burrito in a pan, air fryer or sandwhich press until adequately toasted.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
