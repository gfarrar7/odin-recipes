Vegan Cauliflower Tacos (With Mango Salsa)

These simple vegan tacos easily rival their meat-loaded conterparts!

Cooking Details

Course: Appetizer, Lunch, Main Course

Cuisine: Mexican, Vegan, Vegetarian, Tapas

Prep Time 20 minutes

Cook Time 40 minutes

Total Time 45 minutes (prep salsa whilst cauliflower bakes)

Servings 2 generous servings or 4 appetizers

Roasted Cauliflower Ingredients

  • 1 cauliflower head (chopped into bite-size pieces) or 1 x 500g bag of frozen cauliflower
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 3 large salt reduced chicken stock cubes
  • Juice of 5-6 limes
  • 10 mini soft tortillas (flour tortillas are preferred, however use any you like, such as corn)

Mango Salsa Ingredients

  • 1 large diced truss tomato
  • 1/2 diced red onion
  • 1 large diced mango
  • Juice of 1-2 limes
  • Generous bunch of diced, fresh corriander
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder or onion flakes
  • Sea salt and ground black pepper (to tase)
  • Optional toppings: sliced avocado and corrianger to taste

Instructions

Roasted Cauliflower:

  1. Preheat the oven to 205C / 400F (fan-forced).
  2. In a large, microwave safe, mixing bowl add the olive oil, lime juice and stock cubes.
  3. Heat bowl in microwave in 30 second incriments until stock is soft enough to mix into liquids.
  4. Cut cauliflower into bite size pieces and add to bowl with warm liquid mixture. Toss to combine until evenly coated. (click link to see how to properly prepare/cut cauliflower)
  5. Transfer coated cauliflower to a large baking tray, lined with baking paper, ensuring you save any extra marinade for later. Roast for 40 minutes, tossing half way and recoating in residual marinade.
  6. Once slightly charred and fork tender, transfer cauliflower to large bowl.

Mango Salsa:

  1. Whilst the cauliflower is cooking, dice tomato, red onion, mango and corriander and add to a medium bowl.
  2. To the bowl, add lime juice, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. (Optional: thinly slice 1-2 avocados and tear some sprigs of corriander for garnish).

Construct:

  1. Heat tortillas according to packet instructions.
  2. On individual tortilla:
    • Layer 1-2 spoonfuls of the cauliflower mixture.
    • Layer 2-3 spoonfuls of the salsa.
    • Garnish with whatever sauces/extras you prefer.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
