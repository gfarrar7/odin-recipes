BBQ 'Chicken' Nachos

These Loaded Vegan BBQ 'Chicken' Nachos feature the easiest caramelised BBQ 'chicken', fresh veggies, quick cheese sauce, and a healthy jalapeno-cashew-lime dressing! Add your favorite toppings like corn, green onion, vegan cheese, and more for some EPIC vegan eats.

Cooking Details

Course: Appetizer, Lunch, Main Course

Cuisine: Mexican, Vegan, Vegetarian, Tapas

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2 generous servings or 4 appetizers

BBQ Chicken Ingredients

  • 200-300g plant based chicken style strips (we used the Birds Eye plant based chicken strips in this recipe)
  • 1/4 cup reduced salt BBQ sauce
  • 2 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp reduced salt chicken stock powder
  • 1-2 tbsp garlic powder (to taste)
  • 1-2 tbsp onion powder (to taste)
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs
  • 1 tbsp moroccan seasoning/li>
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose seasoning
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • Pinch of chilli flakes (to taste)

Cashew, Jalepeno and Lime Dressing Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup raw cashews (soaked for 2 hours, rinsed and drained)
  • 1/4 cup corriander (stems removed)
  • 1 jalapeno pepper (seeds and membrane removed, coarsly chopped)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Generous bunch of diced, fresh corriander
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves or 1 heaped tsp minced garlic
  • Sea salt and ground black pepper (to taste)

Nacho Ingredients

  • 1 large diced truss tomato
  • 1/2 diced red onion
  • 2 generous handfuls of sliced lettuce of choice (e.g. iceburg, spinach, cos etc)
  • 1-2 packs of nacho/corn chips
  • Desired amount of vegan cheese
  • Optional toppings: sliced avocado, corriander, jalapenos (to taste)

Instructions

BBQ 'Chicken':

  1. On a low-medium heat, place the 'chicken' chunks in an oiled pan. Using two forks (or your preferred method), shred the chicken chunks until they resemble pulled pork.
  2. To the chicken, add all of the listed sauces and spices and stir to combine until evenly coated.
  3. Cook until sauce is a thickened, caramelised glaze. Take chicken off heat and set aside.

Cashew, Jalepeno and Lime Dressing:

  1. Mix together all ingredients in a high-speed blender or food processor until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour into air tight container and set aside.
  3. (Store your aioli in an air-tight container in the fridge for up to 5 days. It can also be frozen for about 6 months. Your dressing may thicken after freezing. If it does, just mix in a little water until it reaches the consistency you want).

Construct:

  1. Place even layer of nacho chips onto a lined baking tray, ensuring bottom of tray is covered.
  2. On nacho chips, layer:
    • Even spread of shredded BBQ chicken.
    • Even spread of diced tomatoes, onion and vegan cheese.
  3. Bake in oven until cheese has melted and is golden. Remove from oven, top with shredded lettuce and drizzle lime dressing. (Optional toppings: sliced avocado, corriander, jalapenos)
  4. Serve and enjoy!

Enjoy!
