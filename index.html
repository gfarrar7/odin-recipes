ODIN RECIPES - VEGAN

Here are our all-time, 3 most popular Vegan Recipes that will help excite you to cook and eat more plant-based meals at home.

Breakfast Burrito

These loaded vegan breakfast burritos are great for a healthy, protein-packed breakfast (although it is also perfect for lunch, dinner or a hearty snack!). Furthermore, they are easy to make with simple ingredients. Are you tired of eating oatmeal for breakfast? Then try these plant-based savoury breakfast burritos!

Cauliflower Tacos

Authentic Mexican Cauliflower Tacos! Rich and slightly tangy seasoned cauliflower, baked to perfection, stuffed into warm tortillas, and livened up with fresh mango salsa and zesty limes, making for the most delicious and flavorful meal that’s ready in only 45 minutes!

BBQ 'Chicken' Nachos

Looking for a tasty dinner that’s quick, easy and vegan friendly? Loaded with smoky flavour, this BBQ 'chicken' loaded nachos recipe ticks all the boxes. Trust us, this is nacho average recipe!
